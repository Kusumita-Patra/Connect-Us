# Connect Us

## Campus Marketplace for Students

Connect Us is a student-focused marketplace designed to help college students buy and sell items within their campus community. The platform enables students to list products, communicate directly with buyers and sellers, receive notifications, manage carts, and build trust through reviews and ratings.

---

## Problem Statement

Students frequently purchase books, calculators, electronics, lab equipment, and other academic essentials for short-term use. After a semester, these items often remain unused while other students continue purchasing the same products at full price.

Existing marketplace platforms are not specifically designed for campus communities.

Connect Us solves this problem by providing a dedicated platform where students can easily buy and sell items within their college ecosystem.

## System Workflow

1. User registers or logs in.
2. Seller uploads an item.
3. Buyers browse and search products.
4. Buyer views item details.
5. Buyer contacts seller.
6. Buyer and seller communicate through chat.
7. Buyer proceeds with Buy option.
8. Seller and buyer complete the exchange.
9. Buyer confirms the order.
10. Buyer leaves ratings and reviews.
